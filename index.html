About me

I'm currently a Research Associate at Nanyang Technological University in Singapre from 2023.12, supervised by Prof. Bihan Wen. Before that, I got my bachelor & master degree of electronic science and engineering in 2020 & 2023 from Nanjing University (NJU).

My research interests lie in the raw image processing, computational photography, and 3D vision.
